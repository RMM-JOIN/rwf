About Enagic

Founded in 1974, Enagic is a Japanese company recognized for developing premium water ionization technology. The company designs and manufactures advanced water systems that combine high-quality filtration with electrolysis technology.

Every product is engineered with precision and undergoes strict quality control before reaching customers worldwide. Enagic focuses on innovation, long-term reliability, and manufacturing excellence while continuously improving its products through Japanese engineering.

Over Five Decades of Innovation

For more than 50 years, Enagic has continued improving water ionization technology through research, engineering, and product development.

Beginning in Japan, the company expanded internationally and now serves customers in many countries through a worldwide distribution network. Throughout its history, the focus has remained on precision manufacturing, customer support, and continuous technological advancement.

Japanese Engineering Excellence

Every Kangen Water system is manufactured in Japan using advanced production techniques and carefully selected components.

Modern manufacturing facilities combine automated production with detailed quality inspections to help ensure accuracy, durability, and consistent performance. This commitment to precision reflects the high manufacturing standards commonly associated with Japanese engineering.

A Global Brand

Enagic products are distributed through an international network spanning numerous countries and regions.

The company's global presence allows customers around the world to access installation, product information, and customer support through authorized distributors. Its continued international growth reflects increasing recognition of its water ionization systems and manufacturing quality.

Multi-Stage Water Filtration

Before water enters the electrolysis chamber, it passes through a dedicated filtration system designed to reduce unwanted particles while helping preserve naturally occurring minerals.

This preparation stage improves the quality of the incoming water before ionization begins. Clean input water is an important step for maintaining consistent system performance and supporting the electrolysis process.

Electrolysis Technology

Inside the machine, electricity passes through specially designed electrode plates to separate incoming water into different streams.

This electrolysis process changes the characteristics of the water depending on the selected operating mode. The system automatically controls voltage, water flow and operating conditions to produce consistent results.

Platinum-Coated Electrode Plates

At the heart of the system are durable titanium plates coated with platinum. These electrodes are designed to support the electrolysis process while offering long service life and corrosion resistance.

The number and design of the plates contribute to the machine's performance, helping maintain stable operation throughout daily use.

Precision Water Flow

The internal design guides water through filtration, electrolysis and separate output channels in a carefully controlled sequence.

Digital controls continuously manage water flow and operating conditions to help maintain stable performance while allowing users to choose different water modes directly from the control panel.

Designed for Daily Use

The complete system combines filtration, electronic controls, electrolysis technology and durable internal components inside a compact appliance.

Each part is engineered to work together efficiently, providing smooth operation while reflecting the precision manufacturing standards associated with Japanese engineering.

Multiple Water Modes

The Kangen Water system allows users to select different operating modes directly from the control panel. Each mode adjusts the electrolysis process to produce water with different characteristics for specific intended applications.

Changing the mode automatically modifies the electrolysis settings while maintaining a consistent flow through the internal filtration and electrode system.

Different pH Levels

Depending on the selected operating mode, the system can produce water across different pH ranges. The control panel lets users choose the appropriate setting according to the intended application described by the manufacturer.

The electrolysis chamber carefully controls the process to maintain stable output throughout operation.

Simple Digital Controls

A modern control panel provides quick access to the available operating modes and displays the machine's current status during use.

Its straightforward interface is designed to make selecting different water modes simple while allowing users to monitor the system with ease.

Working Together as One System

Every component inside the machine—from filtration and water routing to electrolysis and electronic controls—is designed to operate together as an integrated system.

This coordinated design helps provide stable performance while supporting the different operating modes available to the user.

Designed for Consistency

Rather than relying on a single component, the machine combines precision manufacturing, advanced electronics, engineered water pathways and durable electrode technology into one compact appliance.

This integrated approach reflects the company's emphasis on engineering quality, reliability and long-term performance.
